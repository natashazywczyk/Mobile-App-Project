Some Dog Facts!

Below I am going to list out a few random facts about dogs!

  1. A dog's nose print is like a human's fingerprint, each nose print is unique to each dog

  2. There is a common misconception that dogs are colourlind, they can actually see both blue and yellow

  3. Dogs have an undertsanding of time. If the owner keeps to a routine, dogs can predict when they're to be fed or do routine activities

  4. Dogs can distinguish a change in blood pressure and heart rate in humans, which is why they are great service animals for people with health conditions in that field

  5. There is research suggesting dogs can smell cancer in humans, with them being able to smell the fragrance given off by certain cancer cells. This is still ongoing but it may be helpful to detect cancer early in humans if used to its full capability

  6. Dogs use their tongues to sweat instead of through their skin

  7. For about four months after a pup is born, they are deaf

  8. The oldest domesticated dog breed is Saluki, having been around for thousands of years and originating in the areas now known as Eqypt, Iran and Iraq

  9. Unlike most mammels, dogs actually have three eyelids. It helps to prevent the eyes from drying out

  10. Guide dogs was a concept first introduced in Germany after the Second World War, having the first ever guide dog training school
