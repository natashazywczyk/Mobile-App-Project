Dog History

Did You Know This?

Germany is where the first known domesticated dog originated. However, it is estimated that it was over 135,000 years ago where dogs became more distinguished from the wolf counter-part.

Over thousands of years, through survival of the fittest and through adapting to their surroundings , we see the vast amount of dog breeds across the world. There is thought to be between 200 and 400 different breeds.

Selective breeding by humans had some dog breeds to be bred specifically for certain activies i.e. hunting, fishing, herding. This would mean that there are dogs that have sharper noses than to other, smaller bodyframes or smaller body features.
